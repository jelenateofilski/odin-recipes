Grilled cheese sandwich

Description

This is my favorite snack when I'm not that hungry.

It's quick and easy to make, but tastes really good.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Cover two slices of bread with butter.
  2. Flip them and cover with mayo.
  3. Add the cheese and assemble.
  4. Toast it in a pan until the outside is crusty and the cheese has melted.
  5. Cut in half for presentation and enjoy.
