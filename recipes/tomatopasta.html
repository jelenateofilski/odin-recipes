Pasta with tomato sauce

Description

This is my favourite way to eat pasta.

It's very easy to make, and you can make a lot of it and use it in all kinds of dishes.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion and garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Spaghetti
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Parmigano reggiano

Steps

  1. Bring pasta water to a boil and add pasta.
  2. Saute onion and garlic.
  3. Add canned tomatoes.
  4. Season with salt, pepper and basil
  5. Add the pasta to the sauce and stir well
  6. Serve with cheese and enjoy.
